About us

Riding Under the Sun Riding School

Welcome to Riding under the Sun Centre and Riding School a family run riding centre and one of Spain's premier equestrian establishments. We have been providing a quality equestrian service for over 40 years now and invite you to browse through our site to discover the many facilities and services we offer. The centre is located in suburban Malaga only 50 km from the city centre yet it is located in a very picturesque setting offering magnificent views of Parque Nacional Sierra de las Nieves. From quiet country lanes for the laid-back horseback rider to full cross-country courses for the more adventurous, our instructors are fully trained and experienced. We welcome riders of all levels from beginners to the experienced, including children as young as 12 years old. This is an ideal family activity for those wanting to create lifetime memories.

Spanish Horse Society Approved riding schools have to adhere to the highest standards of equine care and prioritise client safety and satisfaction, so it makes sense to choose a BHS approved stables. Not only has WVS passed with flying colours, but we are also delighted to have been awarded two certificates of excellence. In addition, we are approved by the Association of Spanish Riding Schools and have won their highly regarded ABRS Golden Spur Award for the Instructor of the Year.
