Welcome to Riding under the Sun Centre and Riding School a family run riding centre and one of Spain's premier equestrian establishments. We have been providing a quality equestrian service for over 40 years now and invite you to browse through our site to discover the many facilities and services we offer. The centre is located in suburban Malaga only 50 km from the city centre yet it is located in a very picturesque setting offering magnificent views of Parque Nacional Sierra de las Nieves.
